
Admin:

2 pages - users and Courses

1. Users:

    List of companies, each expandable to show/edit company details and list of people (users)
    Button to add user


2. Courses:

    3 lists in columns:

    1. Certifications:
    Left column
    Expandable to show/edit details
    Buttons to add/delete cert
    Filter/search options

    2. Units:
    Centre column
    Expandable to show/edit details
    Details includes a list of prerequisite units
    Buttons to add/delete unit
    Filter/search options

    3. Content:
    Right column
    Expandable to show/edit details
    Details includes list of assignments (tests, quizzes, etc)
    Buttons to add/delete content (video, slideshow, references, etc)
    Filter/search options


List filtering:

    Clicking a cert will filter units to show cert units
    Unclick to revert to all units

    Clicking a unit will filter to show unit content
    Unclick to revert to all content

In 'all' units mode':
Drag a unit onto a cert to add it
Drag a unit onto another unitto add it as a prerequisite

In 'all content' mode:
Drag content onto a unit to add it
